Clouds

Is it a big puff of mist
or is it magic in the sky
blinding us of what's to come
it's true God has mercy
it can be a blanket of the heavens
A fog that is a blank canvas wanting for us to
use our imagination to make the world a better place
thunder crowding around when we argue
on who is more deserving than the other
but who's to judge
when the rapture will come down
falling at our feet with no choice of despair
